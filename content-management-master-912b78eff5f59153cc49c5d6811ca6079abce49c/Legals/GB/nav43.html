3. Use of cookies

Our website uses cookies. Cookies are small text files, which are dispatched from our web server to your browser as part of your visit to our website, and which will be retained by your browser on your computer for later retrieval.

Session cookies

We use "session cookies" (also known as temporary cookies), in other words, cookies that are saved only temporarily during your use of one of our web pages. The objective of these cookies is to identify your computer during a visit to our website after changing from one of our web pages to another of our web pages and to be able to determine the end of your visit.

The cookies will be deleted as soon as you terminate your browser session.

Tracking and targeting cookies

Our website also uses tracking and targeting cookies, which capture user behaviour. In doing so, we only use "first party cookies", meaning the cookies will not be made accessible to other domains by your browser.

The cookies are used, among other things, for the following services:

  • Range measurement - this function records the number of times that a website is accessed
  • Clickstream analysis - this function records the movements of a user on a web page (e.g. the route that a customer takes to reach a specific product in the shop)
  • Ad Impressions and Click Through - this function analyses how often a given advertising banner is displayed and clicked
  • Mouse movements and heatmaps - this function analyses and captures the mouse movements of users in areas where there is particularly high traffic ("heat mapping")

You can determine if cookies will be placed through your browser settings. For example, you can disable the saving of cookies as such in your browser, or you can limit it to certain web pages, or you can configure your browser to such an extent that you will be notified automatically as soon the server seeks to place a cookie, and you will be asked to respond to this request. However, for technical reasons, session cookies must be allowed to make use of the full range of functions of our website.
